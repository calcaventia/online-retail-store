Style and Elegance Infused with African Cultures





Calcaventia Couture is a South African clothing and accessories brand for women that started operating in March 2021. It provides designer clothing and accessories that are heavily influenced by a mix of African cultures.

"Years ago, before the trend of African prints and traditional clothing picked up, I had dreamt of the day South African people showed up at work, meetings or events in their traditional inspired attires…everyday…with pride.", says the founder and owner, Tintswalo Mabuza.

Calcaventia Couture's African inspired clothing can be worn at weddings, at the beach, on vacation, just to go to the grocery store or even when relaxing at home.
